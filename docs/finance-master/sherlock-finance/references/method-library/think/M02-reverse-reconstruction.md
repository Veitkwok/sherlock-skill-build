# M02 · Reverse causal reconstruction

- **id:** M02  
- **triggers:** fragmented clues, buried footnote, timing mismatch, “what does this add up to”  
- **when_not:** single clean catalyst with full primary disclosure already in L0  

## Method

Do not start from the preferred story. Treat low-signal fragments as pieces of a hidden sequence; work **backward** through time and incentives until the fragments cohere.

## US equities ops

Fragments (Web/IBKR — never invent):

- Related-party / policy change footnotes  
- Buyback or convert timing vs Form 4  
- “Other income” spikes  
- 13F exits vs bullish guidance  
- IBKR options surface skew vs calm spot  
- Capex vs verbal growth story  

Procedure: list fragments → order by date → propose 2–3 reverse chains → falsify each.

## Falsifiers

- Sequence impossible given filing dates.  
- Fragments explained by one ordinary seasonal L0.

## Anti-patterns

- Grand narrative from one odd put print (use IBKR fields only; no dark-pool fantasy).  
- yfinance/longbridge tool calls.

Guidance raise + IBKR heavy call open interest + 13F long reductions → reverse chain may favor financial engineering / distribution over “fundamental inflection” until cash conversion confirms.
