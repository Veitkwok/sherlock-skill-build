# M09 · Role–behavior consistency

- **id:** M09  
- **triggers:** “long-term compounder” vs heavy dilution, strategy flip, buybacks into insider sales  
- **when_not:** no management narrative claims to test  

## Method

Test whether behavior matches claimed role. Stated identity (innovator, capital returner, consolidator) must map to observable capital allocation and disclosure behavior.

## US equities ops

| Claimed role | Expected behavior (L0) |
|--------------|------------------------|
| Compounder | Reinvest FCF; low dilutive SBC trend |
| Capital returner | Buybacks/dividends funded by FCF not only leverage |
| Disciplined acquirer | ROIC / integration metrics; not serial dilution |
| Owner-operator | Form 4 alignment; skin in game |

Compare proxy + 10-K capital section + Form 4 (Web) + optional IBKR reaction to capital actions.

## Falsifiers

- Behavior matches role across ≥2 years of filings.  
- One-time crisis exception fully disclosed.

## Anti-patterns

- Attacking management personality instead of capital mechanics.  
- A5: using charity/ESG as role proof.
